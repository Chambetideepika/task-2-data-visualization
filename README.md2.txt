# Task 2: Data Visualization and Storytelling

## Objective
Analyze Superstore sales data and communicate business insights using visualizations.


## Files Included
- `Superstore_300_Rows_FULL.csv` ( dataset with 300 records ) 

## Visualizations Included
1. **Total Sales by Region**  
2. **Profit by Category**  
3. **Top 10 Products by Sales**  
4. **Discount vs Profit**  
5. **Sales Over Time (Monthly)**  
6. **Key Insights & Recommendations**


## Key Insights
- **West region** has the highest total sales.
- **Technology** is the most profitable product category.
- Discounts generally reduce profit margins.
- A few key products generate most of the revenue.


## Tools Used
- Power BI
